An Aspiring User Experience Designer

Hello, I am a third year student at the University of Toronto Mississauga, specializing in Digital Enterprise Management. Over the course of three years, I have had quite an exposure to user centric and user based designs, where the interfaces are built from the perspective of users. In all of this, the process is the most crucial part as it identifies the design thinking and the research behind it. Displaying this research on paper is very exciting as I get a chance to design prototypes and enhance my skills in various tools and softwares such as Adobe XD. I have experience with planning and coordinating student-led organization and insitution department's events with ambitious teams. I am thrilled to take leadership roles, exploring and improving my communication and oral skills, as well gain experience in areas I have not yet recieved the opportunity to.

Process

Procedure to an Effective User Experience Design

Research + Sketch

Gain knowledge of the criteria and content to design. Create multiple pieces of ideas and drafts.

Prototype + Wireframe

To create middle to high fidelity representation of the final product, which simulates user interface interaction.

Feedback

Ask experts, colleagues, and friends. Re-iterate the prototypes until the best design is constructured.

Visual Design

Understanding the interface through a user's perspective and designing accordingly.
